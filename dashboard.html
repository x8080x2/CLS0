Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a redirect domain?
Simply start a conversation with our Telegram bot, send the /start command, and follow the prompts. You'll need to provide your domain name and the URL you want to redirect to.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Bitcoin (BTC), USDT (TRC20), and USDT (ERC20). All payments are processed securely and verified by our admin team.
How long does domain setup take?
Domain provisioning is automated and typically completes within 30-60 seconds. You'll receive your live URLs immediately after setup.
What's the difference between the templates?
We offer two templates: Plain Redirect Template (simple HTML redirect) and Cloudflare Template (advanced bot protection with Turnstile verification). You can choose your preferred template in settings.
Can I get a refund?
Due to the instant nature of our service, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Please contact support if you encounter any issues.
